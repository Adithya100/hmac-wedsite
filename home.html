What is HMAC? HMAC is a directory of distinct M&A professionals (lawyers, investment bankers, healthcare consultants, accountants, and wealth managers). HMAC reduces transaction costs by managing “requests for proposals” from each professional. For companies looking to sell their business, HMAC can save over $250,000 in transaction fees by picking the right partners while negotiating favorable rates for the Seller. We call this “Transaction Coordination Services.”
